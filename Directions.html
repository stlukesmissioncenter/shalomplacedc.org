Directions to Shalom Place

From I-95 North of Washington, DC…

  • Merge onto I-495 W/Capital Beltway via Exit 27 toward Silver Spring.
  • Take the Connecticut/MD-185 exit- Exit 33- toward Chevy Chase/Kensington.
  • Merge onto MD-185 S/Connecticut Ave toward Chevy Chase.
  • Turn right onto MD-410 W/East-West Highway.
  • Turn left onto Wisconsin Ave/MD-355 S.
  • Continue to follow Wisconsin Ave NW. Turn left onto Calvert Street NW.

 St. Luke’s United Methodist Church is on the left hand side of Calvert Street.

From I-95 South of Washington, DC…

  • Merge onto I-395 N via Exit 170A-B toward Washington/I-495/Tysons Corner. 
  • Merge onto Washington Blvd/VA-27 N via Exit 8B toward Pentagon/Arlington Cemeterey/Rosslyn.
  • Take the ramp toward Pentagon North Parking Entrance
  • Merge onto VA-110 N / Jefferson Davis Highway
  • Turn slight left to take the Wilson Blvd ramp toward Rosslyn
  • Turn slight right onto Arlington Ridge Rd
  • Arlington Ridge Rd becomes 19th Street N.
  • Turn right onto North Lynn Street
  • North Lynn Street becomes Francis Scott Key Bridge
  • Turn right onto M Street NW.
  • Turn left onto 33RD ST NW.
  • Turn slight left onto Wisconsin Ave NW
  • Turn right onto Calvert Street NW.

St. Luke’s United Methodist Church is on the left hand side of Calvert Street.

From I-66 West of Washington, DC…

  • Take Exit 73 toward Rosslyn/Key Bridge
  • Stay straight to go onto Lee Highway/US-29 N.
  • Turn left onto US-29 N/N Lynn Street
  • US-29 N becomes Francis Scott Key Bridge
  • Turn right onto M Street NW.
  • Turn left onto 33RD ST NW.
  • Turn slight left onto Wisconsin Ave NW
  • Turn right onto Calvert Street NW.

St. Luke’s United Methodist Church is on the left hand side of Calvert Street.
