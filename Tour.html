"Do not neglect to show hospitality to strangers, for thereby some have entertained angels." (Hebrews 13:2)

Take a tour of Shalom Place!

Welcome to Shalom Place - a place of hospitality and comfort for your stay in our nation's capital. We hope that you find all the comforts of home while you stay with us and that your experience is transformative as you explore Washington, DC.

Please take a tour of our facility. If you have any questions, please feel free to contact us at 202-333-4949 or by e-mail by clicking here.

Shalom Place is a ministry of St. Luke's United Methodist Church, located in the heart of DC's Glover Park neighborhood.

Shalom Place is equipped with two large dorm-style rooms that can accomodate up to 24 guests. One room can be divided to hold two groups of six people each. The facility has men's and women's restrooms with showers.

Shalom Place has a large kitchen that comes complete with two refrigerators, a stove, microwave, dishwasher, sink and dishwasher. All you have to bring is the food!

Folding tables and chairs are available for you to share meals together and enjoy a fellowship opportunity.

Sit back and relax in the Shalom Place Lounge! Comfy couches and chairs greet you after your day. The Lounge is a great place to reflect on the day's activities or simply hang out. Shalom Place Lounge has TV, DVD, VCR and internet.
 
 
Safe and secure! Shalom Place has a private entrance to which you will have the key. Located right off our parking lot, it's just a quick trip up a flight of steps to the comforts that Shalom Place offers.
